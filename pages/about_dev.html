About Developer

Hello! I’m Nikhil Gugad, a passionate tech enthusiast and aspiring Full Stack Web Developer from Kuchaman City, Rajasthan.

I am currently pursuing CITS (Craft Instructor Training Scheme) in CSA (Computer Software Application) trade from NSTI (National Skill Training Institute), Jodhpur. Before this, I completed my CTS (Craftsman Training Scheme) in COPA (Computer Operator and Programming Assistant) from Government ITI, Kuchaman City. These courses have helped me build a solid foundation in computer applications, programming, and technical education.

From a young age, I have been fascinated by technology and computers. What started as curiosity about how things work soon turned into a strong desire to create, build, and innovate. Over the years, this curiosity has shaped me into a motivated learner who enjoys solving problems, experimenting with new technologies, and bringing ideas to life through code.

I have hands-on experience in front-end web development, where I specialize in HTML, CSS, and Tailwind CSS to design modern, responsive, and aesthetically pleasing websites. My focus is always on creating user-friendly designs with smooth layouts and clean, maintainable code. I love how front-end development allows me to combine creativity with logic — turning static ideas into interactive, visual experiences that users can enjoy.

In addition to these skills, I have a good command of JavaScript, which helps me add interactivity, functionality, and dynamic features to websites and web applications. With JavaScript, I can make designs come alive — from simple animations and event handling to integrating APIs and managing real-time user interactions.

Currently, I’m expanding my knowledge by learning Node.js, Express.js, and databases such as MongoDB and MySQL. My goal is to become a proficient Full Stack Developer capable of building complete web solutions — from intuitive front-end interfaces to powerful, secure back-end systems. This learning journey excites me because it allows me to understand the full picture of how web applications work, from client requests to server responses.

Apart from web development, I also have good knowledge of Office Applications such as Word Processors, Spreadsheets, and Presentation software. I can efficiently create professional documents, data reports, and creative presentations. These skills help me maintain organization, manage information effectively, and present my work clearly.

To blend creativity with my technical expertise, I have hands-on experience in Adobe Photoshop. I enjoy designing banners, posters, UI elements, and graphics that align perfectly with my web projects. Being able to design and develop gives me complete control over how my work looks and feels.

Another strong point of mine is typing — I can type over 70 words per minute with accuracy. This skill may sound simple, but it makes a huge difference in productivity, whether I’m coding, documenting, or managing data.

Beyond academics and work, I always strive to learn new technologies, tools, and frameworks that can improve my workflow. I believe that continuous learning is essential in the tech field, and I’m always eager to explore new concepts, experiment with emerging trends, and take on projects that challenge my creativity and problem-solving skills.

In the future, I aspire to work as a professional web developer, contributing to meaningful projects and helping businesses grow through innovative digital solutions. I also hope to inspire others to pursue learning and technology — just as my mentors and experiences have inspired me.

Thank you for visiting my page and taking the time to learn more about me! Feel free to explore my projects and connect with me through my portfolio. I’m always open to new opportunities, collaborations, and creative discussions.
